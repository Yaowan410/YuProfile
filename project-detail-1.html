SmartDress AI

An intelligent wardrobe assistant that eliminates the daily 'what-to-wear' dilemma using real-time weather data and AI recommendations.

Project Gallery

Project Overview

SmartDress AI is an AI-powered mobile assistant that provides personalized daily outfit recommendations. By combining real-time weather data, personal schedules, and wardrobe insights, it functions as a virtual stylist that helps users select clothes suited to both practical needs and personal style. The app reduces decision fatigue, saves time, and maximizes wardrobe utilization by preventing overuse of certain items and ensuring variety. Designed for busy professionals, students, and fashion-conscious individuals, SmartDress AI addresses the common struggle of "not knowing what to wear," turning an everyday hassle into a convenient and sustainable experience.

Key Features

  • Real-time weather integration for climate-appropriate suggestions
  • AI-powered recommendation engine based on user preferences
  • Calendar integration for occasion-appropriate outfits
  • Wardrobe inventory management with photo cataloging
  • Outfit history tracking and analytics

Problem & Solutions

Choosing an outfit is more complex than it seems, involving personal style, weather, and occasion under time pressure. On average, people spend significant time deciding what to wear—women, for example, spend about 16 minutes daily, adding up to nearly 287 days over a lifetime (Haines, 2009). Yet, about one-quarter of wardrobes remain unworn for over a year (WRAP, 2022), creating both financial waste and environmental impact. With fast fashion and rising sustainability concerns, this gap between abundant clothing and practical outfit choices underscores the need for intelligent solutions that save time, maximize wardrobe use, and support confident self-expression.SmartDress AI is an intelligent mobile assistant that acts as a personal stylist, offering tailored daily outfit recommendations. By integrating real-time weather data, personal schedules, and wardrobe insights, it ensures clothing choices are both practical and stylish. The system helps users save time, reduce decision fatigue, and start each day with confidence. It also maximizes wardrobe usage by avoiding excessive repetition and encouraging variety, making better use of existing clothes. Designed for busy professionals, students, and fashion-conscious individuals, SmartDress AI provides quick, reliable suggestions that align with weather conditions, daily activities, and personal preferences—transforming the everyday challenge of "what to wear" into a convenient, sustainable experience.

User Requirement Gathering and Analysis

Our study focuses on two groups: undergraduates aged 18–22 at the University of Rochester (via WeChat, Discord) and employees aged 23–30 in Rochester earning $40,409–$70,398 annually (via Handshake). Participants selected their top three outfits across styles—Gen Z, retro, formal, and athletic—for different scenarios. Results show a need for a fast, reliable, and personalized outfit tool to ease morning stress and boost confidence. Challenges include too many choices, unpredictable weather, and pressure to stay varied and socially appropriate. Desired features include integrating real-time weather and calendar data with wardrobe management, plus flexible options like quick swaps and reminders. These insights reveal a shared desire for an intelligent assistant that makes daily dressing simpler and more supportive.

User Testing & Results

Tasks:

  • Add new clothes via photo + tags
  • Link calendar events for outfit suggestions
  • Choose style to get inspiration, refine with quick swaps, explore purchase links

Procedure:

  • Pre-test survey on demographics & tech use
  • Test: perform 3 tasks, log clicks/time/swaps, video recorded
  • Post-test interview on satisfaction & ease of use

Data Collected:

  • Completion time, success/error rates
  • Satisfaction ratings (1–5) & feedback

Results:

  • T-test: SmartDress AI satisfaction > Stylebook (T=12.83, p<0.001)
  • Users saved time (10→3 mins), liked inspiration & quick swap
  • Feedback: smooth overall, clothing detection needs improvement

Conclusion:

Viewed as easy, efficient, and stress-reducing. Data collection seen as key to better personalization.
